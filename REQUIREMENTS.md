# Football Scheduler Requirements

## Overview

Generate two separate schedules — one for 7th grade teams and one for 8th grade teams — for an 8-week youth football season.

---

## Schedule Structure

- **Season length:** 8 weeks
- **Season start:** Week before Labor Day (see spreadsheet for exact dates)
- **Games per team:** 7 games + 1 bye week (bye can fall on any week)
- **Divisions:** 7th grade and 8th grade play separately — no cross-grade games
- **Outputs:** Two schedules (one per grade)

---

## Hosting Rules

- All teams must host a **minimum of 3 home games**
  - **Exception: Sauk** — does not have enough available hosting dates to meet the 3-game minimum; schedule as many as possible
- Some schools have **lights and can host 2 games per night**
- Some schools can only **host 1 game per night**
- Each school can only host on **specific days** based on field availability (see Tab 2 of spreadsheet)

---

## Matchup Restrictions

- Teams from the **same school may not play each other**
  - Example: Mt. Horeb Red vs. Mt. Horeb White is **not allowed**
  - **Exception: Sun Prairie East vs. Sun Prairie West is allowed** (and required in Week 4)
- Teams may only play opponents **within their own grade division**

---

## Fixed / Required Games

### Week 1 — August 23 (Saturday Kickoff)
- Verona hosts on their High School field — this is their **only opportunity** to use that field
- Verona hosts **4 games** on 8/23:
  - Verona 7 (Black) vs. Milton 7
  - Verona 7 (Orange) vs. Mt. Horeb Red 7
  - Verona 8 (Black) vs. Milton 8
  - Verona 8 (Orange) vs. Mt. Horeb Red 8
- These count as **Week 1 matchups**

### Week 4 — Sun Prairie Rivalry Week
- **Sun Prairie East must host Sun Prairie West** in Week 4
- Applies to both 7th and 8th grade

---

## Away Availability

- Schools are only available to travel on **specific days** (see Tab 3 of spreadsheet)
- The scheduler must respect away availability when assigning road games

---

## Team-Specific Requests (Honor When Possible)

These are preferences, not hard requirements. The scheduler should try to accommodate them but may not always be able to.

| Team(s) | Request |
|---|---|
| Beaver Dam 7 & Beaver Dam 8 | Travel away on the **same day** (travel together) |
| Monona Grove (has 2 seventh-grade teams) | Play at the **same location** on the same day, OR play on **separate nights** — they share a coaching staff and cannot coach two games simultaneously at different sites |

---

## Summary of Hard Rules vs. Soft Rules

### Hard Rules (must be enforced)
- 7 games + 1 bye per team
- No cross-grade matchups
- No same-school matchups (except SP East vs. West)
- Respect home hosting capacity per night
- Respect home field availability dates
- Respect away availability dates
- Verona 8/23 kickoff games (Week 1)
- Sun Prairie rivalry games (Week 4)

### Soft Rules (honor when possible)
- All teams host at least 3 home games (Sauk excepted)
- Beaver Dam 7 & 8 travel on the same day
- Monona Grove 7th grade teams co-located or on separate nights
